Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our services and platform.

How do I create an account on the job portal?

Creating an account is simple. Click on the "Register" button in the top navigation, fill out the required information, and verify your email address. Once verified, you can start applying for jobs or posting vacancies if you're an employer.

What types of jobs are available on the portal?

We feature a wide range of job opportunities across various industries including technology, healthcare, finance, marketing, engineering, and more. Both full-time and part-time positions are available, with options for remote, hybrid, and on-site work arrangements.

How can employers post job openings?

Employers can create a company account, verify their business, and then use the "Post a Job" feature to list vacancies. You'll need to provide job details, requirements, and application instructions. Our team reviews each posting to ensure quality and relevance.

Is there a fee for using the job portal?

For job seekers, our platform is completely free. Employers can post a limited number of jobs for free, with premium features available through our subscription plans. Detailed pricing information is available on our Services page.
